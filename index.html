What we scan for

The signals most people miss.

📸

Photo age gaps

Date Taken vs Date Modified mismatches reveal recycled or stolen images.

🔍

Reverse image hits

The same photo on multiple unrelated accounts is a definitive signal.

📹

Live video avoidance

Excuses for not doing a real-time video call — a top identity fraud signal.

💸

Money requests

Direct asks, gift cards, crypto, or “investment opportunities.”

⚡

Urgency & pressure

Artificial emergencies, guilt-tripping, and rushing emotional timelines.

🧩

Story inconsistencies

Details about job, location, or background that don’t add up over time.

Risk scale

What your score means.

0–25

Low Visible Risk

No major flags from what you’ve shared. Stay aware — meet in public if you proceed.

26–50

Caution

Some signals worth watching. Don’t send money or meet alone until you’ve verified more.

51–75

High Risk

Multiple serious flags. Stop sharing personal info and do not send money.

76–100

Stop / Severe

This profile matches known scam patterns closely. Disengage safely and report.
